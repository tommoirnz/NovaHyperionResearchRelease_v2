4. 🟡 No Fuzzy / Partial Matching

5. 🟡 No Confidence Feedback

The tool plays whatever it picks with no confidence indicator and no "did you mean...?" mechanism — so you only find out it was wrong when the wrong thing starts playing.

6. ⚠️ Stop Words Applied to Query, Not Filenames
